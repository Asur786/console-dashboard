# Databricks One Notes (Strategic Implications)

## Relevance to This POC

Databricks One directionally supports unified user experience and governance workflows in a way that can strengthen a Databricks-first architecture for analytics applications.

## Potential Benefits

- Stronger unification of identity/governance touchpoints around data-native experiences.
- Reduced context switching for analytics-first stakeholders.
- Better long-term alignment if product roadmap continues toward enterprise app controls.

## Current Gaps for Immediate Decision

- Not all application-layer controls needed by this project are proven in this POC.
- Operational governance still depends on custom API/service controls implemented in this codebase.

## Recommendation Impact

- Databricks One increases confidence in Databricks-first viability over time.
- Near-term decision should still rely on concrete evidence from US2-US4 tests and quality gates.
